Suicide Gang DAO

After a gory suicide mission gone wrong, the Suicide Gangsters are now captured by the Metavillains and injected with their brain frying and memory erasing serum. Most unfortunately, the gangsters forgot how to get back to their hangout along with their secret gang sign in order to be granted access. It is now up to you, and can only be up to you, the holder, to follow and crack the clues in your 1 of 1 Suicide Gangster NFT and help reunite the gang. Once all the codes have been cracked and solved, all holders will be airdropped a free Suicide Gang NFT add-on that regenerates memory revealing the official gang sign and contains more clues regarding the gang's reunion. The first Suicide Gang reunion will be an event inducting all members into the DAO system.
